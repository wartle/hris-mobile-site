Features

Everything employees need, in one app

Purpose-built for the Municipality of Dalaguete's workforce — accurate, secure, and simple.

📍

Geotagged Time Record

Punch in and out in seconds. Every time entry is GPS-tagged and matched to your assigned work site, building your official Daily Time Record (DTR).

👆

Biometric & PIN Sign-In

Unlock the app with your fingerprint or face, or a personal PIN. Your credentials are encrypted on your device — biometrics never leave your phone.

🛰️

On-Site Verification

Geofencing confirms you're at your station, while mock-location detection guards against fake or spoofed GPS for trustworthy attendance.

🗓️

Leave & CDO

File leave applications, check your balances and approval status, and manage Compensatory Day Off (CDO) credits — without a trip to the HR office.

🧭

Locator & Movement

Submit official locator slips and log field movement or travel when you're working away from your usual station.

💳

Payslips & Payroll

View your payslips, earnings, deductions, and loan balances securely, whenever you need them.

🕑

Shift Schedules

See your assigned shifts and schedules so you always know when and where you're expected to report.

👤

Employee Self-Service

Access your profile, service record, eligibility, and HR information any time — your records, in your hands.

Trust & Security

Built for accurate, tamper-resistant attendance

Attendance is an official record. Dalaguete HRIS layers multiple safeguards so every punch can be trusted — and your personal data stays protected.

  • 🔐

    Encrypted on device

    Your PIN and session tokens are kept in the device's secure storage; biometric data is handled by your phone and never transmitted.

  • 🛰️

    Anti-spoofing GPS

    The app checks location integrity and flags mock or fake GPS to keep geotagged attendance honest.

  • 📱

    Device binding

    Your account is tied to your registered device, helping prevent buddy-punching and unauthorized access.

  • 🚫

    Automatic lockout

    Repeated failed sign-in attempts trigger a protective lockout to deter unauthorized use.

Getting the app

Dalaguete HRIS is provided to active personnel of the Municipality of Dalaguete. Access is issued and managed by the LGU.

1

Be an active employee

The app is available to current Municipality of Dalaguete personnel with an HRIS portal account.

2

Install from Google Play

Download Dalaguete HRIS free from the Google Play Store. The ITSM Office or HR Management Office can help if you need it.

3

Sign in & set up

Log in with your portal username and PIN, then enable biometric sign-in on your registered device.
